KARBYTES_JOURNAL_2023_ENTRY_122

I am typing this journal entry while stationed inside of my motel room for one person (as usual) at the Motel 6 in Pleasanton, California. Today I unfollowed hundreds of Twitter accounts and responded to some tweets which some of my favorite Twitter users posted.

I feel guilty about something I tweeted (which is evident in the screenshots below): my method of saving money by essentially stealing rides on the BART train. For now on, I will do my best to refrain from stealing (and that means that I will have to be more disciplined about saving money).

One thing I plan on doing this weekend while stationed at this motel room is first catch up on sleep and relaxation and allow my stomach to become empty. Then tomorrow I plan on consuming the entire half ounce packet of psilomethoxin (allegedly produced from psilocybin mushrooms which were fed 5-MeO-DMT while those mushrooms were growing) which I ordered for approximately $200 (shipping included) from the Texas-based Church of Psilomethoxin. So far, I have never tried any psychedelics other than cannabis (in smoking and in edible form and sourced from various cannabis dispensaries throughout the East Bay region of the San Francisco Bay Area and from cannabis plants which my parents and their friends grew in their backyards) and normal psilocybin mushrooms (in edible and purchased from Zide Door (a.k.a. Church of Ambrosia) in Oakland, California).

For the record, I do not intend to sell drugs to people nor to give drugs away for free. Instead, I intend to acquire and use what I think are relatively safe drugs for my personal use only and to document my experiences related to using those substances on my personal websites (which I think can aid other people in their own psychonautic explorations.
